title: "Full-Stack Product Thinking"
date: "2026-02-14"
summary: "Why I treat engineering, product, and design as one loop."
tags: ["Product", "Engineering"]
---

I want to work where product decisions are close to the code. That is why I enjoy full-stack work. It lets me connect user feedback, metrics, and implementation details without losing context.

A few things I try to practice:

- Ask what the user is trying to get done, not just what they clicked.
- Build the smallest path that proves value and grows from there.
- Keep APIs predictable so the UI stays simple.
- Write notes as I build to explain the why, not just the how.

I am still learning, but this loop keeps me grounded and focused.
